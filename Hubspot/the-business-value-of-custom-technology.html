Complicated problems require customized solutions

Building technology to solve a specific problem requires expertise, flexibility, and patience. It might require network testing, firmware customization, and continued support during the early stages of adoption. With a partner like JACS Solutions, you get all of that and more.

The value of customization can be measured in concrete ways, from cost effectiveness to longer device lifecycles and decreased security risks. When our team is developing a custom solution, there’s a checklist of components we work through, including the following:

  • Compliance Requirements
  • Connectivity Method
  • Device Durability

JACS Solutions has a complete design toolkit, which enables us to build platforms to-spec for all industries. Every design choice is taken into careful consideration, from connectivity to casing. Does the device need a camera? We’ll build it with one. Does it need to be CBRS compatible? We’ll make it happen. Do you want it to be branded with your company colors? Not a problem.

If you need it, we will build it.
