College is More than Classes

Hey, we get it. UMBC is a school that’s better known for its academics than its extracurriculars. But the reality is that with 17 Division I sports teams, 250+ student organizations, and multiple theatre, art, and performance venues right on campus (not to mention Baltimore and DC just a short drive away), there’s always something to keep you busy: 

Take in a Show
From DaBaby to Debussy, there’s a musical event for every taste at UMBC. 

Hang with a Student Org
Enjoy bus trips to DC and Philly, pop-up screen printing booths, festivals, outdoor movies and more.

Explore Other Cultures
UMBC students come from more than 85 countries, so on any given day, you’re likely find cultural celebrations featuring live music, dancing, and–best of all–delicious food.

Cheer on the Team
Get ready to flex your spirit fingers! As a student at UMBC, your cheers are the fuel that helps our Retrievers bring home championships. 

Take a Peek at Campus Life at UMBC ➔

Your Home Away From Home

Living on campus is a great way to make the most of your college experience. You’re right next to the action, plus you’ll find a cozy space to call your own in our res halls (bonus: you won’t have to share your bathroom with the whole floor!).

Plus, UMBC is perfectly situated in Baltimore County between Catonsville and Arbutus. Baltimore, BWI, and D.C. are right around the corner with Internship and job opportunities, music, culture, history, and fun!
